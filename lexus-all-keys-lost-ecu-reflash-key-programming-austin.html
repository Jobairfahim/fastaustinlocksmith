Lexus All Keys Lost – ECU Reflash & Key Programming in Austin

Posted: April 22, 2026 Category: Automotive Locksmith

Losing your only car key can be stressful—but with a Lexus, it can quickly become a complex situation.

Recently, our team at Fast Austin Locksmith helped a customer in Austin who had completely lost their Lexus key. With no spare available, the vehicle was fully immobilized.

Not Just a Key – Advanced Work Required

In many Lexus models, an all-keys-lost situation is not just about cutting a new key. The immobilizer system locks the vehicle, preventing any new key from being programmed until the system is reset.

ECU Access & Reflash

We arrived on-site and carefully accessed the vehicle's ECU (Engine Control Unit).

Once removed, we performed a reflash to reset the immobilizer system. This step clears old key data and allows a new key to be programmed.

This is advanced work that requires proper tools and real experience.

Key Cutting & Programming On-Site

After resetting the system, we:

  • Cut a brand-new key
  • Programmed it to the vehicle
  • Synced everything back together

Everything was completed on-site—no towing and no dealership delays.
