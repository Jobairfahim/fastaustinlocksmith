Rekeying and Lock Change Services

Many homeowners routinely change their locks for security purposes. This technique can help protect against individuals who may have had access to your house key over the course of one or two years. Whether your teenager gave a spare key to a questionable friend, have concerns about a disgruntled ex-roommate or you own rental properties and like to change the locks between tenants, we can help you secure your property. We offer both lock changes and rekeying services for local customers.

Lock Repair and Replacement

Is your lock damaged or dysfunctional? There’s no reason to leave your property exposed to potential theft or vandalism.
