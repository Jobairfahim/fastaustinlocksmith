Can you make a car key without the original key?

Yes. In many cases, a new key can be created even if the original key is completely lost. The process may involve decoding the lock or accessing key information from the vehicle, depending on the make and model.

How long does it take to replace a car key?

Most car key replacements can be completed in one visit. The time may vary depending on the vehicle type, key system, and condition of the locks or ignition.

What types of car keys can you replace?
